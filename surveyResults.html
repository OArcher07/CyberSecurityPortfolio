2023 HHS Survey Results

I prompted other students at Huntley High School to take a survey regarding computer science principles and their basic knowledge about cybersecurity.


Over 150 students have taken the survey which about 5% of the entire school population. Questions inlcuded base-line knowledge about the student, such as their grade, gender, and post high school plans, along with classes they may have taken regarding computer science, what they may already know, and what they would be interested in learning.

Over all, the data shows a better understanding of student knowledge about computer science topics at HHS and what kinds of students are wanting to learn more. Hopefully we can use this information to better advocate for our courses and grow computer programs at HHS.
